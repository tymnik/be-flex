About me

Hello everyone - welcome to my website.


My name is Anastasia and I am a gymnastics coach.

Now I want to tell you about myself and my experience....


I have taught kids gymnastics for more than 7 years. My coaching career started when I was 16 years old. From that time until now I have been so happy to choose this job and enjoy it. Now I am 23 years old and from these 7 years of coaching, I have worked in a lot of different countries: Ukraine, Turkey, Kuwait, and Dubai.

Nowadays I am working in Dubai since 2021 and I like this place, gym and opportunities that I have here.


I work with different ages of kids starting from 4 years old. Also, I have my competition girls with them we visit a lot of competition places in Dubai, Abu-Dhabi, and other cities and they get a lot of medals and trophies.
